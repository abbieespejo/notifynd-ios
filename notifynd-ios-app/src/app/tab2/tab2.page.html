How am I receiving notifications from Notifynd?
Notifynd sends notifications related to participating parties (i.e. businesses, events, services and key locations). If you're sharing your location with Notifynd and you're within a certain radius of a participating party, you will receive a notification!
How does Notifynd benefit me?
Notifynd is a modern platform to discover key information about your nearby surroundings. As the saying goes, "you'll never know until you try it", but with Notifynd you'll learn important information by merely pressing a button!
Why does Notifynd need my live location?
Notifynd uses your geolocational data to detect if you're within a certain radius of a participating party (i.e. businesses, events, services and key locations). Be assured that we do not store your geolocational data, nor do we share it with anyone else.
I don't like the notifications I'm receiving
We're deeply sorry to hear that! Notifynd ensures that all notifications are relevant and beneficial. Please get in touch with us if you have received a notification that you did not like. You can temporarily stop receiving all notifications at any point by turning off location services.
How can I view notifications I've missed?
Notifynd is only available on iOS devices, therefore you should be able to view any missed notifications in your 'Notification Centre'.
