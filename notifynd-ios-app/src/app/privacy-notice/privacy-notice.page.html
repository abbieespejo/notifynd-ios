What is Notifynd?

Whether you're travelling to a new city for the first time or you're a long-time local in your hometown, Notifynd sends you real-time, insightful, bite-sized notifications about the places and businesses around you.

How does it work?

Your phone and Notifynd's technology is able to detect if you're within a certain geolocational radius. As a result, this triggers the relevant notification to be sent to your phone.

What's in it for you?

Notifynd lets you learn about the places around you! Be the first to find out about nearby businesses who are hiring or if there's a music festival not far from you! You're able to bookmark these notifications for future reference as well.

We respect your privacy

We don't store or share any of your locational data. To continue using Notifynd you must allow location tracking.
